WILD IZUŚ (IZABELICUM FERUS S P.)

  • Discovered in 1999
  • Native to Poland but was also spotted in Southern and Eastern part of Europe
  • Doesn't tolerate high temperatures but has a better tolerance of cold climate
  • Usual habitat is a comfortable place, preferably with a blanket and rich in tea and sweets
